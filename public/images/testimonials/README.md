# Testimonial Images

Place your testimonial couple images in this folder.

## Current images needed:
1. `couple1.jpg` - Yehya & Mariam
2. `couple2.jpg` - Hamzeh & Feride
3. `couple3.jpg` - Berivan & Gojko

## Image recommendations:
- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Size**: 400x400px or larger (will be cropped to circular shape)
- **Aspect ratio**: Square works best
- **File size**: Optimize to ~100-200KB per image
- **Subject**: Make sure faces are centered for the circular crop

## How to add images:
1. Copy your image files into this folder
2. Rename them to match the names above (couple1.jpg, couple2.jpg, couple3.jpg)
3. The website will automatically display them

Note: Until you add real images, placeholder images will be displayed.
